Game Help

How to Play

Objective: Successfully smite Baron Nashor when its HP is below 1200.

Controls: Hover over Baron Nashor and press a button to smite.

Scoring: The closer you smite to 1200 HP, the higher your score will be.

Failure Conditions: You will fail if you smite too early (above 1200 HP) or if you let Baron Nashor die without smiting.

Difficulty Specifications

  • Easy: Standard gameplay with no additional mechanics
  • Normal: Fail if HP drops below 1000, HP drops faster when below 2000
  • Hard: Fail if HP drops below 1000, HP drops faster when below 2000, random big HP drops (not near 1200)

Tips

  • Pay attention to the health bar which shows the current HP
  • In Normal and Hard modes, watch out for the HP threshold warning
  • In Hard mode, be prepared for sudden big damage drops when HP is above 1400
  • Adjust latency settings in Options to match your actual game experience
  • Customize your smite button in Options for a more comfortable experience
